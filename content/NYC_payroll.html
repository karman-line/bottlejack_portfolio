Let's create a new column 'gap_pay' to measure the difference b/w regular_gross_paid and total_supposed_pay

If supposed pay is more than gross pay, it means that for some reason, these folks got paid less than they should have. If supposed pay is less than gross pay, for some reason, they got paid more.
